The long story

In the assignment we picked Talvipuutarha as our conceptual client, so we collaborated on crafting a marketing and social media strategy for the existing greenhouse located in Helsinki.

The project encompassed the design of print advertisements (flyers) and various social media posts. We also developed dedicated website content for the Talvipuutarha, paying careful attention to the brand we created and search engine optimization keywords.

Process

Our approach involved leveraging personas and mood boards to build a distinctive brand identity, selecting colors, and establishing a thematic framework. This comprehensive project not only showcased my proficiency in marketing and social media strategy but also highlighted my ability to create a cohesive and visually appealing brand identity for the assignment.

The process included benchmarking, brand sprinting and moodboard making.

Results

I gained valuable insights from the project, particularly in creating and implementing brand identity. The experience taught me how to effectively utilize personas and mood boards in projects, and enhance the use of search engine optimization tags and keywords. Additionally, it provided ample opportunities to hone my visual design skills.
