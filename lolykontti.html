Löylykontti

Social media strategy, visual identity guidelines, Instagram guidelines, an annual social media calendar and ready-made templates with text content

With Löylykontti, a unique sea container sauna offering single private sessions and subscription-based services, our client sought to establish a robust social media presence.

The project included the creation of a comprehensive social media strategy, content ideas for Facebook and Instagram, a meticulously crafted social media marketing calendar with key dates and preloaded campaigns, and adaptable templates for posting content on both platforms.

The long story

Teaming up with Löylykontti, a unique sea container sauna service, our client aimed to enhance their online presence. So we crafted a comprehensive social media strategy, provided content ideas, and designed a detailed marketing calendar with adaptable templates.

This included clear visual identity guidelines, 40+ customized templates, an annual social media calendar, and instructions for effective usage for the client to use.

Process

Our team started by developing a clear visual identity guidelines form the clients logo and brand colors, setting the stage for a cohesive online presence. From this we fashioned over 40+ templates tailored to the client's needs and preferences.

I made an annual social media calendar, thoughtfully divided into a comprehensive yearly view and a detailed day-by-day breakdown, incorporated all major holidays and occasions, providing a structured framework for engaging content. In addition our team equipped the client with ready-made templates featuring text content, along with guidelines detailing optimal posting practices on Instagram and Facebook. I also included instructions on leveraging Canva for effortless social media post editing for the future to the client. To further facilitate ease of use, we curated an image bank, suggesting diverse ideas for capturing attention-grabbing visuals for their social media channels.

Our dedication to smooth collaboration was evident in our project management, utilizing Miro for weekly planning, fostering a collaborative environment for idea generation and analysis. Crafting the social media calendar in Google Sheets involved meticulous iterations. For image creation, Canva, Adobe Photoshop, and Illustrator were employed. Our ultimate aim was to empower the client in seamlessly using provided templates through Canva for long-term success beyond the project.

The design process included benchmarking, Crazy 8, brand sprinting and persona making.

Results

This project enriched my skills in crafting a cohesive brand identity and optimizing online presence. Initiating with clear visual identity guidelines and designing over 40 templates, I learnt to use practical applications of design principles. Creating an annual social media calendar with major events and holidays, along with ready-made templates and usage instructions, highlighted my strategic approach to content planning.
