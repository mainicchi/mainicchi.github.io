A little about me

Name's Maini Tran and I'm currently advancing through my last year as a digital design student at Metropolia University of Applied Sciences.

I pride myself on my proactive approach to challenges and striving for the best in my work. I aim for usability and accessibility in the end product. I also want to bring creativity and functionality together to create designs that leave a lasting impact. Additionally, I possess an understanding of front-end coding.

My journey in the design realm has been marked by a commitment to continuous learning and growth. I am enthusiastic about delving deeper into the field of UX/UI design and to always learn something new.

Outside of work I enjoy photography, cocktail making and hosting crafting nights for my friends.
