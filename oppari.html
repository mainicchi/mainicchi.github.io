Digital Design Bachelor Thesis

The possibilities of using Artificial Intelligence in User Interface Design: How ChatGPT and DALL-E can be used in UI Design

Artificial intelligence is changing how designers produce content. The purpose of this thesis was to investigate how AI programs such as ChatGPT and DALL-E can be utilized in UI design. The theoretical section of the thesis covers the background of AI, key concepts, and OpenAI programs. Additionally, the thesis discusses the background, principles, and methods of UI development, and explores the utilization of artificial intelligence in UI design, as well as their background relating to each other.

Process

The study was conducted as a design task and through semi-structured interviews, involving three individuals with expertise in UI design and knowledge of AI functionality. The results of the case study were compared to the UI design principles presented in the thesis.

Results

The study revealed that ChatGPT can be useful in user interface design. The program helped to condense and provide new ideas for UI design, and facilitated a better understanding of user needs and how to design UIs that meet those needs. On the other hand, DALL-E was more directional than precise as a tool for designing the visual appearance of the UI. It provided helpful guidelines for the designer but may not be a precise tool for implementation of the design.
