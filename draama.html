Drama gallery

Reform the page structure and content of the website. Additionally unified the look and content of the clients social media channels

Revamping Drama Gallery, a Finnish party wear collection, involved a transformation aimed at enhancing the website's page structure and content.

Leveraging the client's existing WordPress framework, we sought to unify the brand's visual identity across various platforms, including Facebook and Instagram.

I also developed a tailored social media strategy for the client, accompanied by detailed instructions for effective Instagram usage. Furthermore, the project incorporated an on-site training session, where we provided hands-on guidance to assist the client on Instagram usage.

The long story

The website underwent a comprehensive update, involving the renewal of both page structure and content, as well as the unification of the appearance and content across the company's social accounts on Instagram and Facebook.

The design steps and ideation driving this transformation were orchestrated by our team at the factory, drawing inspiration from service design principles and research insights.

In addition to the website upgrade, our involvement extended to offering light consulting on digital marketing strategies. This included providing insightful guidance on optimizing the company's presence on Instagram and Facebook, ensuring alignment with the company's specific needs and leveraging available resources most effectively.

Process

Form the start of the project the client wished to use the company's existing WordPress site template. The client contributed to the project's visual side by providing photos and other relevant materials, fostering a collaborative approach.

Our journey commenced with a deep dive into understanding the customer target groups, followed by a thorough exploration of the WordPress theme in use this being new to the team. Benchmarking against different online shops and employing card sorting techniques for user paths informed the creation of a comprehensive sitemap. We translated this data into wireframes using Figma, incorporating light user testing for refinement.

The iterative design process continued with the construction of visual prototypes, rigorously tested and refined based on user feedback. The final design was seamlessly integrated into the WordPress platform, ensuring a cohesive user experience.

The culmination involved rigorous testing of the new design, implementing adjustments based on feedback, and compiling comprehensive instructions. This project underscores our commitment to delivering holistic solutions, seamlessly blending website revamps with strategic social media enhancements.

Results

Beyond the website overhaul, our project extended to optimizing the client's social media presence. I crafted a social media calendar for scheduled posts, devised a strategic plan to elevate marketing efforts, and even provided ready-to-use story posts. As a hands-on addition, I conducted an on-site training day, guiding the client through effective Instagram utilization.
